Jazan

Start your journey to explore this charming coastal region, whose beaches extend for 200 kilometers on the Red Sea coast.

Here is Jazan...It is characterized by amazing geographical, environmental and cultural diversity

Let us reveal interesting experiences for you, starting with diving, and ending with climbing the Al-Qahr Mountains and the green mountains of Faifa. The region is known for producing the finest types of tropical fruits, such as: mango, Bananas, papaya, and figs. Jazan allows you to enjoy the picturesque nature of the Farasan Islands, which are registered within the Man and Biosphere Program of UNESCO, and the region includes historical monuments dating back to 8,000 years BC. It has multiple aspects of life, inhabited by modern life, while the villages highlight a way of life full of authentic traditions. The people of Jazan are characterized by a captivating simplicity, and they receive their visitors with great hospitality and kindness. They also adhere to an impressive traditional culture, as their heritage is colored in bright colors, evident in their fashion and urban patterns, and their heads are decorated. People with sweet white jasmine flowers. In Jazani cuisine, you find the most delicious local cuisine in Saudi Arabia, as you taste fresh ingredients expertly cooked in traditional tools, producing extremely delicious dishes.

Farasan Island

It is a nature reserve with enormous biodiversity and is famous for its many mangrove trees and parrot fish. On this island you can discover many adventures for diving enthusiasts, and among the most famous diving spots on this island are Shib, Amar, Marmar, Dahra, Gorgolian Point and many others.

Fifa Mountains

The Fifa Mountains, or as they are called “the neighbor of the moon,” are distinguished by their charming view, at a height of more than 1,800 km above sea level. They are a series of mountains wrapped around each other, with peaks covered with greenery.

Qayar village

If you want to see a piece of heaven, visit the most beautiful tourist place in the Jazan region.

Al Khouba hot springs

One of the famous tourist places in Jizan is the Al-Khubah hot springs, which are located 75 kilometers from the city center. In these springs you can jump into the warm water and get rid of many diseases such as physical and mental stress. In addition, you can benefit from all the natural minerals found inside the springs.
